fun with the web

One Size Fits Nose

One Size Fits Nose explores storytelling through a messaging interface to explain the concepts of anticipatory design and intelligent interfaces. The project foretells a world where our technology knows what we want before we ask for it.

Metereol: A Weather Visualizer

Metereol is a typographic weather visualizer. Instead of just displaying quantitative weather measurements, it generates a dynamic representation of how it feels outside based on live data. The project was published as a Chome extension.

Massage Me

In the spirit of Marshall McLuhan & Quentin Fiore’s rapid-fire text The Medium is the Massage, each click returns a quote by McLuhan and a mashup of Flickr Commons images.

How to be Creative

Celebrating the randomness of the Internet, every click returns Google search results for a string of three randomly generated words. The spontaneous combinations of terms open up distant parts of the web and stimulate creative thinking.
